Hooticus is a virtual assistant designed to improve student engagement by providing quick, helpful answers to common questions, simplifying access to important information about academic programs, events, and resources. While working as an Honors intern, I was inspired to create this chatbot using Node.js, Express.js, OpenAI's API, HTML, and CSS to combine my passion for computer science with my commitment to helping honors students.

iPod Revampnorth_east

Auri is a voice-controlled music player inspired by the classic iPod, reimagined for Gen Z. Built for ColorStack’s Y2K Rewind hackathon, this demo lets users play music with their voice, customize the interface, and enjoy an accessible listening experience. I collaborated with my team to develop this demo using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and the Deezer API. We also integrated speech recognition and to create a fully interactive experience for all users.

Screen Time Management Appnorth_east

Proxima is a screen time management app designed to encourage healthier digital habits. For KSU’s Apps for Wellbeing Hackathon, our team developed the concept of using a mobile app to support wellbeing by helping users reduce screen time. We used Mendix to build the app, focusing on AI-generated activity suggestions, behavioral nudges, and progress tracking. The app also includes goal setting and a progress tracker to help users stay motivated.
